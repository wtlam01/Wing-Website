About the Animation Internship

During my internship at HRB Education, I worked as an animation intern creating educational motion graphics for the organisation’s YouTube channel. The videos are designed to support visual learners by explaining complex topics through clear, engaging animation.

I assisted across the full animation workflow — from early concept development and storyboarding, to asset preparation, animation, and final delivery. The project required careful attention to pacing, clarity, and accessibility to ensure the content remained educational and easy to follow.
