About the Internship

During my internship at Moor Beer Company, I collaborated with the design and marketing team to produce animations and visual media for product promotions. This experience strengthened my skills in animation timing, camera movement, and integrating 2D artwork into motion and video workflows. I worked across After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Maya.
