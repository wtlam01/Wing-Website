About the Internship

During my internship at Moor Beer Company, I collaborated with the design and marketing team to produce animations and visual media for product promotions. My main tasks included animating beer can label designs, editing promotional videos, and creating engaging motion graphics for social media campaigns.

This experience allowed me to develop strong skills in animation timing, camera movement, and integrating 2D product artwork into 3D environments. I worked extensively with tools like Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Maya.
