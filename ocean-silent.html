When the Ocean Fell Silent

Embodied Projection Installation — Ocean Noise & Whale Communication

When the Ocean Fell Silent is an interactive projection prototype designed to help visitors understand how ocean noise pollution disrupts whale communication. Visitors’ shadows become whale silhouettes, and the system responds in real time to ambient sound.
