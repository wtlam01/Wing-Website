After building the physical object, I extended the project into interactive media. I designed a mobile game in Adobe XD that turns the reconstructed calculator into a character inside a whac-a-mole style game. The physical object becomes the in-game interface and visual motif.

The prototype explores screen flow, user states and simple interactions. Players tap to ‚Äúwhack‚Äù the character, while the interface keeps the playful, handmade feeling of the original object.

Alongside the game prototype, I created a stop-motion animation featuring the physical toy. The animation combines frame-by-frame motion and sound design to bring the character to life and imagine how it could exist in a narrative context.
