🐰 Project Overview

This animated video aims to raise awareness about the ethical concerns of animal testing. It opens with a thought-provoking role-reversal scenario and transitions into real-world data, laws, and encouragement to support cruelty-free options.

🎯 Message

To encourage critical reflection on animal cruelty in science and inspire viewers to consider more ethical alternatives.

🛠️ Tools Used

Procreate (Illustration), Adobe After Effects (Animation), Adobe Illustrator (Infographics), Audacity (Sound Editing)
