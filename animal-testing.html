Animal Testing Animation

Awareness Through 2D Motion Graphics

This animated video raises awareness about the ethical concerns of animal testing. It begins with a thought-provoking role-reversal concept, then transitions into key facts, legislation shifts, and a call-to-action encouraging cruelty-free choices.

Message

Encourage critical reflection on animal cruelty in scientific testing, and promote more ethical alternatives.

Tools Used

  • Procreate — Illustration
  • Adobe After Effects — Animation
  • Adobe Illustrator — Infographics
  • Audacity — Sound Editing

Workflow

From ideation to storyboard and shot planning (based on my production report).

  • Ideation mindmap + research direction
  • Moodboard to lock visual tone
  • Voice-over script drafts to control pacing
  • Storyboard to plan narrative flow
  • Shot list to organise scenes and timing
