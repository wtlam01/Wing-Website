Bowling Mischief

3D Rigging & Character Animation · Audio-Visual Storytelling

Bowling Mischief is a short 3D animation exploring comedic timing and character interaction. The story follows a mischievous boy who sneaks up in a bowling alley and throws a ball towards an unsuspecting man, causing him to fall.

Project Objectives

  • Practise character rigging and walk cycles
  • Develop believable body movement through weight painting
  • Explore comedic timing using camera cuts
  • Enhance storytelling with sound design

Tools & Software

  • Maya — Character rigging & animation
  • Weight Painting & Walk Cycle setup
  • Adobe Premiere Pro — Audio editing & final cut
