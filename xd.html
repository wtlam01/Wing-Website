Bear’s Factory

Customize-your-own bear • Adobe XD mobile prototype

Bear’s Factory is a customizable teddy bear retail app prototype. Users can choose the body shape, color, facial features, clothing, and accessories, preview the bear instantly, and add it to the cart. The prototype was created in Adobe XD with interactive flows.
