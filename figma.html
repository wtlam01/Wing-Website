Project Overview

InMotion is a well-being mobile app designed for university students to stay active and boost body positivity. The app encourages users to join movement challenges, track emotions, and celebrate progress.

Goals

  • Enhance body confidence through non-weight-related movement
  • Motivate students to stay active in fun, stress-free ways
  • Promote emotional awareness alongside physical activity
