Project Overview

For the Media Studio — “Making it” brief, I filmed a 30-second piece that turns a simple craft into a tiny story: using old magazines and a chocolate-strands bottle, I fold an origami crane, lucky stars, and assemble a small wishing bottle.

Focus

Clean framing, close-up textures, rhythmic cutting, and gentle sound to make a mundane process feel calm and satisfying.

Tools

Adobe Premiere Pro
