Project Overview

The Dream Pillow is an interactive concept object designed to help users relax and prepare for sleep. Inspired by the idea of combining comfort with light and sound elements, this pillow responds to touch and creates a calming bedtime environment.

Tools Used

Ideation Sketching, Photography, Adobe After Effect, Adobe Premiere Pro
